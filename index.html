Our story

Built in Daytona.
Sharpened on the mats.

Insomnia Jiu Jitsu is a Daytona Beach gym for people who take this seriously — and for people who've never set foot on a mat. Whether you're here to lose weight, learn self-defense, sharpen your edge for competition, or just find something better than scrolling at night, you'll find a room of training partners who actually want you to get better. We believe technique beats strength, attention to detail beats ego, and a welcoming room beats every other gym in town.

Programs

Find your room.

Four ways to train at Insomnia. Every program is open to every member — start where you're comfortable and grow from there.

Adult BJJ — Gi & No-Gi

A complete program for adults of any experience level. Drill fundamentals, learn techniques layered for retention, then test them in live rounds with partners who match your pace.

All levels · Mon–Fri

Coaches

Meet the team.

Three coaches. One philosophy: technique over force, detail over ego, growth at every level.

JG
Black Belt · Head Instructor & Owner

Jessie Grace

Jessie built Insomnia from the ground up in Daytona Beach. Ten-plus years on the mats, decorated competitor, and a coach known for careful, detail-driven instruction that meets every student where they are. Safety, technique, and steady progress are the cornerstones of how she teaches.

Gold · IBJJF Pan Ams Bronze · No-Gi Worlds Silver · Abu Dhabi Pro No-Gi (Black) Gold · IBJJF Memphis
SM
Brown Belt · Coach

Sadiq Muhammad

Sadiq is the technician of the room. He breaks positions down to the smallest details and rebuilds them step by step until you actually feel the lever click. Patient with new students, sharp with advanced ones, and quietly responsible for half the breakthroughs that happen here.

DW
Purple Belt · Coach

David Welle

David is the energy of the gym. He remembers what your first month felt like and coaches like it. Newer students gravitate to him because he makes the hard parts feel doable — and the wins feel earned. Growth mindset by default, every class, every round.

Competition team

If you want to compete,
we'll get you there.

Insomnia's competition team trains alongside the regular program with sharper expectations: harder rounds, structured drilling, and real coaching through tournament season. We compete IBJJF, NAGA, and local Florida events. You don't need to be a phenom to join — you need to be coachable, consistent, and willing to lose well before you win.
